Internship Overview

Aspirova Technologies offers comprehensive internship opportunities for UG, PG, and Diploma students. Our internships aren't about shadowing or passive learning—you will join a structured project group and contribute code, designs, or analysis to a real technical pipeline.

Under the instruction of senior engineering mentors, you'll learn how to navigate professional version control, manage agile sprint cycles, and structure features according to industry standards.

Stream Alignment

Tailored project pipelines for Computer Science, Electronics, Mechanical, and Civil streams.

Live Development

Build modules, debug systems, and execute tests on active codebases.

Professional Workflows

Learn Git, Jira, Agile sprint planning, and code review processes.

Experience Credentials

Walk away with a verified internship certificate and performance review.

Technical Domain Tracks Available

Choose an internship track that aligns with your branch and future career goals:

CS / IT & MCA Stream

Full-Stack Web Dev (React & Node), Python App Dev, Cloud computing modules, and AI model integrations.

ECE / EEE & Telecom

Embedded systems, Internet of Things (IoT) protocols, MATLAB designs, and digital circuit simulation.

Mechanical & Automobile

CAD modeling using AutoCAD & SolidWorks, finite element analysis (FEA), and thermal stress testing.

Civil Engineering

Structural analysis, drafting blueprints in AutoCAD Civil 3D, and material load estimating.

Internship Timeline

1
Onboarding & Setup

Choose your domain, complete initial setups, and get access to the codebase/tools.

2
Foundation Sprint

Work on basic features under mentor guidance to understand coding/design protocols.

3
Core Sprints

Take ownership of modules, participate in standups, code reviews, and testing.

4
Submission & Review

Deploy features, review code contributions, and receive your official internship letter.

Quick Facts
  • Hands-on active projects
  • Duration: 1 to 6 months batches
  • Weekly standups and review milestones
  • Formal internship letter included

Project Guidance Overview

Completing final year projects can be challenging. We support students from B.E., B.Tech, MCA, M.Tech, and Diploma programs to successfully implement their academic projects. Our mentors guide you step-by-step through technology stacks, assembly protocols, and testing phases.

We don't just hand over code—we ensure you understand the architecture, design choices, and data flows, preparing you to answer any question from external academic examiners with confidence.

Technology Selection

Choose from IEEE standards, IoT, AI/ML, Cloud, Robotics, Embedded Systems, or structural CAD designs.

Documentation Support

Professional guidance to write your abstract, design synopsis, and final report.

Code & Hardware Assembly

Hands-on support to build the working software or wire hardware components.

Viva Voce Mock sessions

Simulated examinations to test your knowledge and polish your project presentation.

Academic Project Verticals

We support standard and advanced IEEE-based projects across core branches:

AI / ML & Software Projects

Image processing, prediction models, deep learning nets, and full-stack web/mobile application architectures.

IoT & Robotics Projects

Sensor networks, smart agriculture systems, automation rigs using Arduino, Raspberry Pi, ESP32, and node hardware.

Power & Control Projects

MATLAB simulation projects for renewable energy systems, battery management, and smart grid applications.

Mechanical & Civil Simulation

Finite element modeling, CFD thermal analyses, CAD structural setups, and load stress validations.

Project Implementation Roadmap

Topic Selection & Approval

Choose from our library of IEEE topics or propose your own idea. Get abstract/synopsis ready for college approval.

2

3
Build & Code Integration

Conduct active coding or hardware wiring sessions guided by your project mentor.

4
Documentation & Mock Viva

Get your final report print-ready, understand every line of code, and clear simulated mock viva checks.
